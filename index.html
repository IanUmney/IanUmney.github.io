Current Interest

Blockchain Oriented Funding For International Needs (BOFFIN)

My most recent area of interest, particularly within the realm of technology, has been the transparency and accountability in the funding and delivery of humanitarian aid.


This focus stems largely from my prior involvement with, and current understanding of, aid organisations and individuals, as well as my awareness of the associated hardships and challenges they encounter.


In this regard, I have been researching the efficacy of implementing blockchain technologies to enhance the transparency and efficiency of these processes.
