About

The first goal of TrustAid is to gather evidence against World Aid Runners to satisfy that they have, and are acting in the interest of the foundation.

The future of TrustAid is that of ensuring that, through smart contracts and blockchain technologies, trust, transparency, even funding is possible and accessbile to all humanitarian aid charities and volunteers.

The need for transparency and future technologies in public affairs is essential in the modern day. It has always been my aim to help as many people as I can, however passively.

This has previously meant restraining myself against official actions such as my involvement with Spirit of Ukraine, in consideration to the effect my actions would have had.

There must be accountability. This recent incident has made be focus once again on bringing accountability to aid charities and volunteers who have displayed signs of untrustworthiness.


Future Plans

TrustAid (Trusted) will demonstrate the ability to incorporate blockchain technology and smart contracts as a layer of trust and accountability with direct donations, and charity engagement. The decentralised nature of the blockchain and activity verification methods, allow a strong foundation for essential operations for humanitarian aid.
