TrustAid BOFFIN

Blockchain-Oriented Funding for International Needs (BOFFIN) is my project, aimed at providing scalable, technologically driven solutions to the operational and logistical challenges faced in humanitarian aid.

The goal is to ensure that the public can track every penny of their donation and guarantee it reaches those who need it most.

The next six months will be dedicated to research, testing, implementation, and, ultimately, the delivery of the blockchain system for use by the general public.

This page will be regularly updated with relevant information.

The Challenge

  • Billions are donated, but trust is low due to lack of visibility.
  • Many people fear their money won’t reach the cause.
  • War zones like Ukraine make transparency even harder.

The Solution

A public, blockchain-powered ledger that records every donation, milestone, and proof of impact — in real time.

  • Donations in any currency
  • Transparent campaign tracking
  • Immutable, verifiable audit trail

How It Works

1. You Donate

Using our payment portal

On payment, you create a smart contract with the blockchain with the parameters you set.

2. Blockchain Record

Every donation is logged on the blockchain and is tamper-proof.

Because it is decentralised, anyone can view it.

3. Upload Proof

Photos, receipts, and reports are stored via IPFS.

This ensures users can always access it, and tampering is clearly visible.

4. Track Impact

Donors can see the results in real time.

As a trusted person, you can also help to verify data on the blockchain.

Why Blockchain?

  • Permanently visible, tamper-proof records
  • Open verification for anyone
  • No single point of failure
  • Builds trust with donors and partners
