Trust Where It Matters Most

Transparent Aid for Real Impact

We help donors, volunteers, and aid organizations track every dollar and ensure it reaches the people who need it — no crypto required.

The Challenge

  • Billions are donated, but trust is low due to lack of visibility.
  • Many people fear their money won’t reach the cause.
  • War zones like Ukraine make transparency even harder.

Our Solution

A public, blockchain-powered ledger that records every donation, milestone, and proof of impact — in real time.

  • Donations in any currency
  • Transparent campaign tracking
  • Immutable, verifiable audit trail

How It Works

1. You Donate

Use your method of choice — bank, PayPal, crypto, etc.

2. Blockchain Record

Every donation is logged on the blockchain, tamper-proof.

3. Upload Proof

Photos, receipts, and reports are stored via IPFS.

4. Track Impact

Donors can see the results in real time.

Why Blockchain?

  • Permanently visible, tamper-proof records
  • Open verification for anyone
  • No single point of failure
  • Builds trust with donors and partners

Real Use Cases

  • Fund a medical clinic in eastern Ukraine
  • Track aid to displaced families
  • Proof of supplies delivered to volunteers
  • NGO accountability through transparent reporting
