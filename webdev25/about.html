What is John doe?


A "John Doe" is a placeholder name used to refer to an unidentified or generic person. It is often used in legal contexts, such as in court cases or medical records, to protect the identity of individuals involved.


In the context of this project, John Doe refers to a fictitious person whose personal information is generated for testing purposes. This includes details such as name, date of birth, address, banking information, IP, driving license, national insuranc, and other identifying information.


It is important to note that the information generated is not real and should not be used for any illegal or unethical purposes. The use of John Doe in this project is solely for educational and testing purposes.
