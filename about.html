Ian Umney

Who is this daft twat?

I was born in the north of England in '93 and moved to the south towards the end of the decade.


My family and I moved to New Zealand in 2000, Australia in 2005, and Germany in 2008.


I returned to the UK in 2011 and bounced around from place to job until traveling around Europe in 2015.


In 2016, I travelled to Ukraine and met my wife. We married in 2019 and welcomed my son Jonathan later that year.


We lived in Ukraine until the Russian invasion in 2022 where, after being out of the country at the time, had to rescue them from their hometown.


I blogged my journey on TikTok and went globally viral during the first days of the war.
